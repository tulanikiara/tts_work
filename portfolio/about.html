about Tulani!


Hi! I'm a Wellesley College Media Arts and Sciences graduate. I love creating and reading heartfelt stories. I love how technology has made art and storytelling more accessible. One of my favorite instances of this is webcomics! I'm working on developing my own right now called NEFT!! Video games, interactive fiction, and visual novels are also forms of storytelling I love.
